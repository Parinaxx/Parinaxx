Upcoming Events

2023 Freestyle National Team Trials

August 29 - August 30 2022. Beachburg

Halifax Canoe and Kayak and the Canoe Kayak Freestyle Committee are excited to announce a partnership with the Ottawa Kayak School / Wilderness Tours to host the 2023 National Team Trials, August 29th and 30th (2022), in Beachburg, Ontario
The 2023 ICF Freestyle World Championships, taking place June 27th to July 1st, in Columbus, Georgia, will once again be hosted on a wave feature, which makes the Ottawa River and Ottawa Kayak School, host of the 2015 ICF Freestyle World Championships, the perfect place to train and compete for a spot on the 2023 National Team.

2022 ICF CANOE SPRINT AND PARACANOE WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS

August 3 - August 7

Lake Banook rejoins the global stage in Summer 2022 as the most powerful paddlers on the planet come to Nova Scotia to battle it out. The 2022 ICF Canoe Sprint and Paracanoe World Championships will take place from August 3–7, in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia. With a dynamic race schedule on the lake, and a free public cultural festival along Prince Albert Roadway, this international event is sure to entertain.
